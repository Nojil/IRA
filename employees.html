Taking the next steps with IRA

We believe that good, honest, hard working people, should have the “American Dream” opportunity to grow and improve their quality of life. And because of our commitment to this belief, we are excited to offer you and your family the chance to live, work, and persevere in the United States through a permanent residence, what is commonly referred to as Green Card.

Green Card

A Green Card is your golden ticket into the United States. This allows you to legally and permanently immigrate to the United States to start living your dream. Green Cards are for a period of 10 years and can easily be renewed for additional 10 year terms. Once you have been in the United States for 5 years, IRA can help you attain permanent citizenship.
